# The DOL Method — Product Context

## Product Purpose
Premium digital education program teaching specialist physicians in LATAM (Mexico, Colombia, Argentina) to become Digital Opinion Leaders (DOLs) recognized and contracted by pharmaceutical companies. Trains the creator skills (Instagram, LinkedIn, AI workflows, Reels) that make pharma select you — not just the theory.

## Register
Brand. Marketing landing page. Design IS the product. First impression determines conversion.

## Users
Specialist physicians, ages 30–50, LATAM (primarily Mexico City, Bogotá, Buenos Aires). Already know what a DOL is. Want to develop digital creator skills and AI workflows to become one. Busy — clinic + hospital schedules. Skeptical of generic marketing coaches. Respond to precision, insider knowledge, and LATAM-specific expertise. Scrolling on mobile after clinic hours, dim apartment, looking for a way to build an income stream that doesn't require more hours.

## Brand Voice
Direct, confident, insider. Spanish LATAM (vos/usted mix by country). No fluff. Results-first. Never says "en este programa aprenderás…" — shows the system, not the promise. The founder was the evaluator — that asymmetric credibility is the entire moat.

## Founder Credibility
Milan: 5+ years at Servier ANCAC (Mexico, Colombia, Ecuador, Central America, Caribbean) in Business Excellence and Digital Marketing. Literally applied the scorecards that selected DOLs. Now teaching from the other side.

## Anti-References
- Generic medical marketing coaches (patient acquisition focus)
- Purple-gradient SaaS landing pages
- TikTok doctor influencer aesthetic
- Anything that looks like "healthcare + teal"
- Motivational speaker vibes
- Glassmorphism by default

## Strategic Principles
- Insider knowledge as primary moat (temporal: maximum value 2026–2029)
- Creator skills framing first; pharma recognition as the outcome, not the pitch
- LATAM specificity is a feature (compliance, platform behavior, pharma market)
- Compliance knowledge = trust signal, not a checkbox
